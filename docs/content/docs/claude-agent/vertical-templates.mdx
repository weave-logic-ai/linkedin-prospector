# Vertical Templates

The agent ships with researched ICP configurations for 15+ industry verticals. Each template provides complete `icp-config.json` settings ready for use with `configure.mjs generate --json`.

## Cross-Vertical Patterns

### Signal Categories

Buying signals on LinkedIn fall into five universal categories:

| Signal Category | Where Found | Examples |
|----------------|-------------|---------|
| **Pain signals** | Headline, About | "struggling with", "looking to improve", "need help with" |
| **Initiative signals** | About, Experience | "launching", "implementing", "migrating to", "evaluating" |
| **Growth signals** | Headline, About | "scaling", "expanding", "growing team", "series A/B/C" |
| **Role-change signals** | Experience dates | New role in last 90 days = budget cycle reset |
| **Technology signals** | Skills, About | Specific platforms, tools, or methodologies |

### Universal Behavioral Indicators

These LinkedIn profile patterns indicate high engagement regardless of industry:

- **Pipe-separated headlines** ("CEO | Speaker | Advisor") -- socially savvy
- **"Helping" language** in headline -- sales-minded, open to conversations
- **500+ connections** -- networkers who accept outreach
- **Creator mode enabled** -- active on platform, responsive to DMs
- **Multiple group memberships** -- community-oriented

### Role Hierarchy by Deal Size

| Deal Size | Buyer (Budget) | Influencer (Recommends) | Implementer (Uses) |
|-----------|---------------|------------------------|-------------------|
| < $10K | Director, VP | Manager, Lead | IC, Analyst |
| $10K-$100K | VP, SVP, C-suite | Director, Sr. Manager | Manager, Lead |
| $100K-$500K | C-suite, President | VP, SVP | Director, Manager |
| > $500K | CEO, Board, CFO | C-suite, SVP | VP, Director |

## SaaS Sales Rep

An Account Executive or SDR selling a horizontal B2B SaaS product to mid-market and enterprise buyers.

**Recommended weights:** `roleWeight: 0.30, industryWeight: 0.25, signalWeight: 0.30, companySizeWeight: 0.15`

**Key profiles:** Enterprise Software Buyer (CIO/CTO/VP IT), Mid-Market Champion (Director IT/Head of RevOps)

**Key signals:** digital transformation, cloud migration, evaluating solutions, RFP, vendor selection, tech stack consolidation

**Key niches:** enterprise-it, revops, mid-market-tech

## LinkedIn Profile Signal Taxonomy

Signals that predict engagement and openness to outreach, organized by detection method:

### Headline Patterns
- Pipe-separated roles: `CEO | Advisor | Speaker`
- Helping language: `Helping [persona] achieve [outcome]`
- Credentials listed: `MBA | PMP | AWS Certified`

### About Section Signals
- Connector language: "helping", "building bridges", "connecting"
- Speaker/author: "keynote", "published", "podcast"
- Builder language: "building", "scaling", "grew from X to Y"

### Experience Signals
- Recent role change (< 90 days): budget cycle reset
- Multi-company tenure: broad industry knowledge
- Board/advisory roles: network multiplier

### Connection Signals
- 500+: active networker, accepts outreach
- Creator mode: responsive to DMs
- High mutual count: warm relationship potential
